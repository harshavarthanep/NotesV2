💡 How ZenDocs works

Writing & saving

Type in the page; it saves automatically. The status shows "Saving…" then "Saved", and the live word count sits beside it.

The toolbar (left → right)

  • Undo / Redo and Format painter
  • Font, Size, Styles
  • B / I / U / S, text & highlight color
  • Lists — bullets, numbers, checkboxes
  • Indent, line spacing, quote, code, align
  • Link, image, note, clear formatting

Links, slash commands & more

Highlight text and press the link button in the toolbar — the URL is applied to that text as a hyperlink. Bare URLs you type turn blue and clickable automatically; click a link to edit/open/remove it, or Ctrl+Click to open directly. Type / on a line for Notion-style block commands (headings, lists, checklist, quote, code, today's date…). The 🧭 Outline in the Notes panel lists this note's headings — tap to jump. Download offers Markdown (.md) export that's Obsidian-ready. In Graph view you can drag nodes by their name too, pan by dragging empty space, and zoom with the buttons or scroll wheel.

Workspace views (V3.1)

The row of buttons at the top of the sidebar opens full-screen views: ☀️ Today opens (or creates) today's daily note, 📅 Month is a calendar — tap any day to jump to that day's daily note, 📋 Board is a kanban board where you drag notes between To do / In progress / Done (◀▶ buttons work on mobile), 🎨 Canvas is a freeform board — add text cards or note cards, drag them anywhere, and connect them with 🔗, and 📑 Tmpl holds built-in templates plus any note you save as a template. While typing, an autocomplete popup appears after [[ (note titles) and # (existing tags) — navigate with ↑↓ and insert with Enter or Tab.

#Tags, [[links]] & graph

Type #tag anywhere in a note — it becomes a clickable tag and shows up in the Tags list in the sidebar. Type [[Note Title]] to link to another note (Ctrl+Click to follow on desktop, tap on mobile — if the note doesn't exist yet you can create it in one tap). The Linked mentions section in the Notes panel shows every note that links to the one you're reading, and the Graph view (🕸 in the sidebar) draws your whole vault of notes, tags, and links.

Notes & comments

Select text and tap the speech-bubble to attach a note. Signed-in users can add and resolve notes. Guests can view but not add notes.

Folders

Double-tap a folder name to rename it. Long-press or right-click to delete.

Exporting

Download gives you Word (.doc), PDF, or plain text. Shortcuts: Ctrl/Cmd + Z / Y undo & redo, Ctrl/Cmd + S save, Ctrl/Cmd + K search, Ctrl/Cmd + . zen mode, Ctrl/Cmd + G graph view.
