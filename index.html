💡 How ZenDocs works

Writing & saving

Type in the page; it saves to the cloud automatically about a second after you stop. The status under the title shows "Saving…" then "Saved", and the live word count sits beside it. Sign in anywhere to find your notes.

The toolbar (left → right)

  • Undo / Redo and Format painter — copy formatting from one place and paint it onto another.
  • Font — Sans, Arial, Times New Roman, Georgia, Garamond, Courier, Verdana and more.
  • Size — type a number or use − / + to step.
  • Styles — Normal text or Heading 1–3.
  • B / I / U / S, text & highlight color.
  • Lists — bullets, numbers, checkboxes.
  • Indent − / +, line spacing, quote, code, align.
  • Link, image, note, clear formatting.

Notes & comments

Select any text and tap the speech-bubble to attach a note. The text is highlighted; anyone with access can read the notes in the Notes panel. On a shared link, viewers can add notes too.

Margins ruler

On desktop, drag the markers on the ruler above the page to change left and right margins. Margins are saved with the note.

Checklists on mobile

Tap directly on a checkbox to tick or untick it. Scrolling won't toggle it by accident, and quick repeat taps are de-bounced so a box won't flip twice.

Folders, Important & Sharing

Group notes in folders or star them as Important. Tap Share to copy a read-only live link; edits sync to viewers in real time.

Exporting

Download gives you Word (.doc), PDF, or plain text. Shortcuts: Ctrl/Cmd + Z / Y undo & redo, Ctrl/Cmd + S save.
