💡 How ZenDocs works

Writing & saving

Start typing in the page. Everything saves to the cloud automatically about a second after you stop — the status under the title shows "Saving…" then "Saved". Open the same account on any device and your notes follow you.

The toolbar

The toolbar stays pinned above the page, so it never scrolls out of reach. Left to right:

  • Font & Size — switch between Sans, Serif, Mono, Georgia, Garamond, Arial and four text sizes.
  • Heading — turn a line into a large, medium, or small heading.
  • B / I / U / S — bold, italic, underline, and strikethrough.
  • Color swatches — text color and highlight color.
  • Lists — numbered, bulleted, and tappable checkboxes.
  • Quote & Code — block quote and code block.
  • Align — left, center, right, justify.
  • Link / Image / Clear — insert a link or image, or strip formatting.

Checklists on mobile

Tap the checkbox list button, then tap directly on a box to tick or untick it. Scrolling won't trigger a toggle by accident.

Folders & Important

Group notes into folders from the sidebar, or star a note as Important to find it fast. Deleting a folder keeps its notes — they move back to All Files.

Sharing & live sync

Tap Share to copy a read-only link. Anyone who opens it sees your edits update live. If they save a copy to their own account, their copy keeps syncing from yours too.

Exporting

Use Download for a Word (.doc) file with your alignment and styles preserved, or Print / PDF to save a clean A4 PDF through your browser's print dialog.

Handy shortcuts

Ctrl/Cmd + B / I / U for bold, italic, underline. Ctrl/Cmd + S forces a save. The word count sits under the title.
